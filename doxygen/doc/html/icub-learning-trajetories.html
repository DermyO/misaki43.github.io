iCub-learning-trajectories

This program is a subpart of the project "icub-learning-trajectories" composed of different programs:

  • recordTrajectories.cpp: to record into some files (recordX.txt, were 'X' is your new trajectory) the icub's arm movement as following: "X_geom Y_geom Z_geom Fx Fy Fz mx my mz x_robot y_robot z_robot" where the trio values are: the position given by the geomagic, the forces and moment received by "wholeBodyDynamicsTree", and the cartesian position of the icub simulated robot.
And two matlab program to learn the distirubtion other these trajectories and to infer the end of an initiated movement:
  • demo_plotProMPs.m: It plots the different results (observed trajectories, learned distribution, infered trajectory from few samples);
  • demo_replay.m combined with replayTrajectories.cpp: It replays learned and predicted movements on the simulated robot.
